What is Ascend?

Ascend is a knowledge and mentorship platform built exclusively for the Rajagiri community. It bridges the gap between where you are and where you want to be — by connecting you with seniors, alumni, and faculty who've navigated the exact same path. Every question, resource, and connection on Ascend is grounded in real experience from within your institution.
