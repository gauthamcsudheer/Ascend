About Ascend

Ascend is a platform where Rajagiri's students seek guidance from the people they trust — their seniors, alumni, and faculty. As a faculty member, your academic expertise and institutional knowledge make you a vital part of this ecosystem. Students turn to Ascend for the kind of clarity that goes beyond lectures — career direction, research advice, and subject guidance from voices they already respect.

How you can contribute

Guide students with answers

Students ask questions about academics, research opportunities, and career paths. Your perspective as faculty carries a weight they can't get elsewhere.

Share academic resources

Contribute reference materials, recommended readings, or study guides. Resources curated by faculty are among the most valued on the platform.

Connect across departments

Students from any department can seek your guidance. Accept connection requests to open a direct channel for mentorship, research discussions, or academic advice.
